# Program

The institute is organized as a three-week training arc.

## Week 1: Neuroimaging Foundations

Lead: **Fez**

Topics include brain anatomy, MRI terminology, BIDS, preprocessing, phenotype extraction, quality control, and precision functional mapping.

## Week 2: Genomics and Statistical Genetics

Lead: **Saonli**

Topics include genetic QC, GRM, population stratification, ancestry, heritability estimation, GWAS, and polygenic risk score modeling.

## Week 3: Integrative Analysis and Reproducibility

Lead: **Lin**

Topics include study design, data harmonization, PRS validation, reproducible outputs, scientific documentation, and capstone projects.
